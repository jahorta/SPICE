## Opcode 17: Set Bit

**Description**

```text
Sets a bit in the bit field at 0x80310b3c

*hex[*add[*flr[*div[<Offset>,8]*]*,*int[80310b3c]*]*]*

*hex[*mod[<Offset>,8]*]*

*bit[*hex[*mod[<Offset>,8]*]*]*
```

**Notes**

